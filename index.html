About Me

I am a postdoctoral researcher in (multivariate) algorithmics at the University of Bergen, where I obtained my PhD in 2020. Between September 2021 and February 2022 I was on a leave from this position as a postdoc at the University of Warsaw. My main research interests revolve around graphs in algorithms, in particular algorithmic and structural aspects of width measures and graph classes. I am furthermore interested in connections of width measures to other mathematical domains.
