About Me

I am an assistant professor in informatics at NHH Norwegian School of Economics. Prior to that, I held postdoctoral positions at the IT University of Copenhagen, the University of Warsaw, and the University of Bergen, where I obtained my PhD in 2020. My main research interests revolve around graphs in algorithms, in particular algorithmic and structural aspects of width measures and graph classes.
